IVD Web Optimisation

Your IVD website should work as hard as you do.

We have reviewed hundreds of websites of IVD manufacturers, distributors, and research organisations. Most of them share the same problem: they tell the company's story, but they don't solve the visitor's problem.

When a potential client lands on your site, they are not looking for your biography. They come with a specific need: a reagent, a test system, a regulatory solution, or a partnership.

If your website does not immediately answer their question — or worse, if they cannot find the answer at all — they leave. And they rarely come back.

We have worked with IVD companies across Europe and Asia. We know the language of immunoassays, clinical chemistry, PCR, and regulatory affairs. But we also know how to translate that expertise into a digital experience that converts visitors into leads.

What we offer:

  • Audit — we analyse structure, content, user flow, and technical performance of your existing website.
  • Full website development from scratch — built for IVD companies, with clear product catalogues, application pages, and technical documentation.
  • Content strategy — we help you articulate what makes your product unique, not just what it does.
  • Conversion‑focused design — every page answers the question: “Why should I choose you?”

Your website is not a corporate brochure. It is a tool for solving your client's problems.
We make sure it works.
