About

Thinking about expanding your reagent portfolio or moving beyond a simple buy‑sell model — but don't want to spend years on in‑house development? There is a solution: technology transfer.

With 30 years of expertise in both IVD development and supply, we unlock new opportunities. We save companies years of effort and hundreds of thousands of dollars in investment by offering a proven, ready‑to‑implement technology transfer package — and we back it with a reliable supply chain of validated components to scale your business.

All you have to do is bring the finished product to your market. And even there, we can support you — by building a website, analysing the competition, and solving the tough integration challenges of deploying your solution into the complex architecture of real‑world laboratories.

We are an international, multidisciplinary team with deep expertise across the entire IVD landscape. Whether you need ELISA, CLIA, clinical chemistry, PCR, rapid tests, histology staining, or urine and faecal diagnostics — we have the know‑how. We accompany you from the very first idea through to implementation, market launch, and commercial support.

T3/T4 immunoassay systems

High‑quality T3/T4 antibodies, dehormonized human and bovine serum, BSA‑conjugated antigens and Poly Apo HRP — everything you need to build a world‑class thyroid diagnostic kit.

One‑component TMB solutions

Ready‑to‑use TMB substrates for HRP detection: four formulations for ELISA, automated platforms, and high‑throughput screening. Stable, sensitive, and consistent.

Magnetic beads

Carboxyl, tosyl, epoxy, NHS and streptavidin‑coated magnetic beads. Designed for chemiluminescent immunoassays, immunoprecipitation, and nucleic acid extraction.

Biological buffers for any application

A full spectrum of biological buffers: HEPES, MOPS, TRIS, CAPS, MES, PIPES and more — for ELISA, clinical chemistry, PCR, cell culture, and protein stabilisation.

A broad range of antigens and antibodies for your application

From native and recombinant antigens to monoclonal antibodies against small molecules, biomarkers, toxins and allergens — we supply the key reagents for your IVD development.
