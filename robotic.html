Modular ELISA Workstation

Engineering for Precision and Scale — high‑performance modular ELISA workstations for clinical and industrial laboratories.

Our platform combines advanced photometry, precise liquid handling, integrated washing, and orbital shaking in a single, space‑efficient system. Built for high‑volume workflows, it delivers consistent results with minimal operator intervention.

System Architecture

Chassis and Layout

  • Three‑section design: lower cabinet, working deck, and overhead module.
  • All modules are interconnected via a unified CAN/Ethernet network.
  • Central control unit — an industrial PC developed for real‑time operation.

Working Deck

  • 12 microplate positions with full walk‑away capability.
  • 4 independent liquid dispensers for flexible assay setup.
  • 2 integrated washers (8‑channel heads) with enhanced pressure control.
  • 6 orbital shakers with temperature control (ambient to +125°C, 100–1800 rpm).
  • On‑deck tube storage — stainless steel racks for 24 tubes each.

Photometer

  • Wavelength range: 405, 450, 492, 620 nm (expandable to 6 filters).
  • Absorbance range: 0–4 OD.
  • Accuracy: ≤ ±1% and 0.005 Abs up to 2.5 Abs.
  • Linearity: ≤ ±0.5% from 0.1 to 1.5 Abs; ≤ ±0.75% from 1.5 to 2.5 Abs.
  • Read time: ≤10 seconds per plate.
  • Liquid level detection, clot and foam detection — standard.

Liquid Handling

  • Air‑displacement pipetting with disposable tips (200 µl and 1000 µl).
  • Dispensing volume: from 50 µl.
  • Tip racks: 104 tips per rack, with proprietary coding for supply chain control.

Buffer and Waste Management

  • 2 buffer reservoirs — 15 litres each.
  • Waste tank — 15 litres.
  • Level monitoring on all positions with visual and audible alerts.

Software and Connectivity

Control and Data Management

  • Embedded web‑server for instrument control — no external PC required.
  • Touchscreen interface with real‑time status display.
  • Support for remote diagnostics and firmware updates.
  • Built‑in statistical data collection and trend analysis.

Plate Generator Software

  • Workflow management for high‑load assignment and batch processing.
  • Flexible plate layout design and protocol definition.
  • Real‑time monitoring and automated scheduling.

LIS Connectivity and PCR Options

  • Standard LIS integration for seamless data exchange and reporting.
  • Optional PCR module for thermal cycling and real‑time amplification.
  • Unified software interface for both ELISA and PCR workflows.

Metrology and Compliance

  • Instrument validation package submitted to national metrology institute.
  • Calibration protocols and performance verification procedures are standardised.
  • Designed to meet clinical and industrial quality requirements.
